## The Business Story

Organicaf is a family business with a rich history. Derived from the larger company "SOBACAF" (founded in 1996), Organicaf specializes in 100% organic coffee and traditional spices. The business is built on:

- **Generational expertise** - Torr√©facteurs (coffee roasters) from father to son
- **Quality commitment** - Artisanal roasting methods
- **Natural products** - No chemical additives
- **Traditional values** - Hand-selected spices and organic coffee beans

## The Challenge

The business needed a complete e-commerce solution that would:

- Showcase their products effectively
- Handle online orders and payments
- Support multiple product categories (coffee, spices, machines, accessories)
- Maintain their brand identity and story
- Reach customers across Morocco

## Technical Solution

### Platform Choice: WordPress + WooCommerce

I chose WordPress with WooCommerce for several reasons:

- **Flexibility** - Easy to customize for their specific needs
- **E-commerce capabilities** - Robust shopping cart and payment processing
- **Content management** - Easy for the business to update products
- **Scalability** - Can grow with the business
- **Local support** - Well-documented and supported in Morocco
